Phase 0 — Core Awareness

Goal: find the diaphragm; selectively recruit the transversus abdominis (TA) without breath-holding or pelvic tilt.

Diaphragmatic Breathing High

Coordinate breathing with core stabilizers and reduce sympathetic arousal.

Open guidance

Abdominal Drawing-In Maneuver (ADIM) Medium

Preferentially recruit TA with normal breathing.
